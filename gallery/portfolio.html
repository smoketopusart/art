Hi! I'm Patters, or Smoketopus arts!

My work tends to feature a lot of bright colors, mechanical-inspired designs with a rounder, cartoony feel! I am fairly experimental with what I make, while I work heavily digitally (clip studio paint), many of my favorite pieces began as acrylic paintings, and I have been experimenting with creating traditional animation cels.

The pieces at my table will vary via event, but I am primarily interested in fanart at the moment. I have a mix of prints, buttons, stickers, stationary (leatherette journals, reusable stickerbooks), keychains, 3d mousepads, and hand-made books and zines.

I have been tabling since 2019, though I started tabling regularly around 2022, at transformers-themed conventions, and a few local anime conventions.

Thank you for checking out my stuff, and if you have any questions, feel free to email me.
